Text Diff Checker

💡 How to use this tool:

  • ✏️ Enter or paste your original text in the first textbox.
  • 📝 Enter or paste your modified version in the second textbox.
  • 🔍 Click Compare to highlight the differences:
    • Inserted text will be highlighted with green or underline.
    • Deleted text will appear with a strikethrough.
  • 🔄 Use Reset to clear both textboxes and start over.
