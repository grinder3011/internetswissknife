Unit Converter

💡 How to use this tool:

  • 🔄 Select the Category of units you want to convert (Length, Weight, Volume, Time).
  • 🔢 Enter a single number/decimal number or enable Batch Mode to convert multiple numbers at once (one per line).
  • 🔧 Choose your preferred Decimal Separator (period or comma) based on your locale.
  • ▶️ Click Convert to see the converted values across different units in the selected category.
  • 📋 Use the Copy Results button to copy the output to clipboard for easy pasting elsewhere.
  • ⬇️ In Batch Mode, you can download the results as Text, CSV, or JSON files.
  • 🔄 Use the Reset button to clear inputs and results to start fresh.
