How to Use This Tool

  • 1. Enter your text: Type or paste your content into the input area at the top. Any code or HTML will be ignored for safety.
  • 2. Get instant stats: The tool will automatically display word count, character count, sentence count, and number of unique words.
  • 3. Use toggles to refine output: You can choose to ignore punctuation, count/exclude spaces, and filter short words from unique word count.
  • 4. Convert your text: Apply case transformations like UPPERCASE, lowercase, or Title Case to your original text.
  • 5. Export results or text: Use the copy/download buttons to save either the results or your original/formatted text.
  • 6. Reset anytime: The reset button clears all input and output for a fresh start.
