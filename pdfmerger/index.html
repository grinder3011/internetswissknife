Simple PDF Merger

💡 How to use this tool:

  • 📁 Drag & drop or select multiple PDF files to upload them.
  • 🔗 Click Merge PDFs to combine them into one file.
  • ⬇️ Once merged, click Download to save your PDF.
  • 🔄 Use Reset to start over.
