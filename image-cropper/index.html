🖼️ Simple Image Cropper

💡 How to use this tool:

  • 📂 Upload an image using the file input at the top of the page.
  • ✏️ Click "Draw new crop area" to select the region you want to crop.
  • 🧹 Use "Clear crop area" if you want to reset and draw a new one.
  • 🔍 Use Zoom In (➕) and Zoom Out (➖) to adjust the image view if needed.
  • ✂️ Once you're happy, click "Crop" to preview your selection.
  • 🗂️ Choose your desired format (PNG or JPEG) from the dropdown.
  • ⬇️ Finally, click "Download" to save your cropped image.
